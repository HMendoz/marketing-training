Try it yourself

Take a piece of paper and brainstorm this out, considering what you're selling with the piece of writing you've selected to workshop. Whatever it is, don't think about everything you sell, or want to sell; think about the sales goal of the one specific thing you want to write right now. (Again, this doesn't have to be financial; the goal may be to get someone to opt-in, or to read more, or to vote in your favor, or to even email you back.)
